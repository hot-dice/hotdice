Rules

  • Hot Dice has 2 scores to track, the running score and total score.
  • A player rolls 6 dice to start and must retain at least 1 scoring die per roll.
  • They may end their turn any time and transfer their running score to the total score or continue to roll their remaining dice.
  • If no scoring dice are rolled, the player BUSTS, ending their turn and losing their running score.
  • If all dice score, the player has HOT DICE! and gets to roll all 6 dice again while maintaining their running score.
  • This process is repeated until a playerâ€™s total score is 10,000.

Boxcars - six dice combinations

  • Six of a kind of 1s is 4000 points
  • Six of a kind is 3200 points
  • A straight (1,2,3,4,5,6) = 3200 points
  • Three pairs is 3200 points
  • Two triple sets is 3200 points

Scoring roles less than six dice

  • Five of a kind = Die value x 300 points
  • Four of a kind = Die value x 200 points
  • Three of a kind = Die value x 100 points
  • Single 1 = 100 points
  • Single 5 = 50 points
